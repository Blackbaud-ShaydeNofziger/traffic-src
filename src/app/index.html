Bobby Earl Traffic I hate traffic. Now you can too.

I sit in traffic a lot... and I hate it. I wanted to very quickly get a real-time view of the cameras provided by 511sc.org. Their website is also very useful, but requires clicking on individual cameras to load, similar to the map view on my sight. I wanted to see more than camera at once, which is why this site was born.

The current version of this site is built using many of the Open Source tools provided by my current team and others at Blackbaud. Specifically, SKY UX Components, SKY UX Builder, and Stache.

 Heads up! The camera feeds are currently available via RTMP and HLS, both of which require Adobe Flash Player in most desktop browsers. For the best user experience, I suggest enabling Flash for this site. High Five
